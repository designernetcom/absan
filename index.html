Welcome to

Absan Polymers

We, Absan Polymers, were founded in 1996 at Wagholi, Pune. Our company specializes in Plastic Injection Molding. We also deal in plastic suspension parts for truks, trailers, bogiee. We have a team of engineers and designers with extensive processing knowledge, as well as a variety of advanced machines. These Injection Moulded Plastic Components are employed in many sectors because of their features including simple operations, durability, low maintenance, and high performance.
